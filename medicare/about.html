About MediTrack

A modern pharmacy management experience for customers and store teams.

Our mission

MediTrack connects patients with genuine medicines and gives pharmacies a clear view of inventory, orders, and stock health — all in one simple interface.

What we offer

  • • Online medicine catalog with category filters
  • • Cart & checkout with delivery details
  • • Login / register for customer accounts
  • • Pharmacy dashboard for sales & stock alerts
  • • Prescription (Rx) product highlighting

Why MediTrack?

Built for clarity: brand-led home experience, fast product discovery, and a management dashboard that keeps low stock visible.
